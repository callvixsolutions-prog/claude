Why Callvix

Built for one industry, staffed by real people, priced to be fair

Callvix exists to help pest control companies capture every opportunity that comes through the phone. We're a newly launched, founder-led company — which means you work directly with the people building this, not a support queue.

What makes us different

Four things most answering services can't say

Real people, not a bot

While the market chases AI, we stay human — because urgent pest calls need judgment and warmth a robot can't fake.

Pest control only

We don't dabble across ten industries. Our agents are trained on pest terminology, urgency, and booking — nothing else.

Answered in your name

Never "thank you for calling the answering service." Customers only ever hear your company — you're the brand, always.

Fair, flat pricing

A flat $2.99 per call — not per-minute meters that punish you in busy season. And no long-term contract, ever.

Why we exist

Missed calls quietly cost pest control businesses real jobs

In an industry where urgency and responsiveness decide who wins the job, a missed call is usually a lost customer. Callvix operates as an extension of your office — answering in your company name, following your rules, and handling customers professionally while your team stays on the truck.

We start by learning how your business handles calls today: your services, service areas, hours, and booking rules. From there, we apply your exact workflow to every single call we answer — never a generic script.

You stay in the field. We stay on the phone.
How we deliver quality

A trained team, held to U.S. business standards

Like many U.S. service companies, Callvix uses a globally distributed team to keep coverage consistent and pricing accessible — without a long-term contract locking you in.

Trained for pest control

Every agent is trained on pest control terminology, urgency levels, and your specific service structure before taking a single call.

Clear, professional communication

Agents are selected for clear English, strong listening skills, and a professional phone presence, so callers get a seamless experience.

Oversight on every call

Calls are monitored, outcomes reviewed, and workflows refined over time — efficiency never at the expense of quality.

We're transparent about how we operate: our team includes agents based in Cairo, Egypt, selected for strong English fluency and customer-service experience — the same delivery model many U.S. service businesses rely on to offer broad coverage at fair pricing. Our focus isn't where the team sits; it's how every call is handled — under your rules, in your name.

Where we are today

We're newly launched — and that works in your favor

Callvix is onboarding its first pest control partners now. That means founder-level attention on setup, no bureaucracy, and pricing built to prove value before you commit to anything long-term.

Hands-on onboarding

You work directly with the team setting up your account — your rules, hours, and pricing configured with you, not by a script.

No long-term contract

Try Callvix without locking into a multi-year agreement. We earn the relationship every month.

Built to grow with you

As one of our first partners, your feedback directly shapes how the service evolves — coverage, reporting, and integrations included.
