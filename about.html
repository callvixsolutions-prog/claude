Our approach

Built for one industry, so we're good at exactly one thing.

Callvix Solutions exists to help pest control companies capture every opportunity that comes through the phone. We're a newly launched, founder-led company — which means you work directly with the team building this, not a support queue.

Why we exist

Missed calls quietly cost pest control businesses real jobs

In an industry where urgency and responsiveness matter, missed calls often mean missed jobs. Callvix operates as an extension of your office — answering calls in your company name, following your rules, and handling customers professionally while your team stays focused on the field.

We start by understanding how your business handles calls today: your services, service areas, hours, and booking rules. From there, we apply your exact workflow to every single call we answer — not a generic script.

Your workflow, not a script

We map your services, hours, and booking rules before the first call is ever answered.

Defined processes, every time

Call intake, booking, routing, and follow-ups all follow the same defined process.

Monitored & refined

Calls are monitored for quality, and workflows are refined over time for accuracy and consistency.

Who this is for

The people doing the job stay on the job

Every process we've built starts from the same picture: a technician who's on a roof, under a house, or already at the next stop — not standing next to a ringing phone.

That's who Callvix is built for. While your team is out treating properties, we're the ones picking up, asking the right questions, and getting the job on the calendar.

Built for U.S. pest control businesses
How we deliver quality

A global team, held to U.S. business standards

Like many U.S. service companies, Callvix uses a globally distributed team to keep coverage consistent and pricing accessible — without a long-term contract locking you in.

Trained specifically for pest control

Every agent is trained on pest control terminology, urgency levels, and your specific service structure before taking a single call — not a generic call-center script.

Clear, professional communication

Agents are selected for clear English communication, strong listening skills, and a professional phone presence, so customers experience a seamless interaction.

Oversight on every call

Calls are monitored, outcomes are reviewed, and processes are continuously refined — efficiency never comes at the expense of quality.

We're transparent about how we operate: our team includes call agents based in Cairo, Egypt, selected for their strong English fluency and customer-service experience — the same delivery model many U.S. service businesses rely on to offer extended coverage at accessible pricing. Our focus isn't where the team sits — it's how every call is handled, under your rules, in your name.

No long-term contract
