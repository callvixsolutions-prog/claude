Pricing

Flat, honest pricing that doesn't punish you in busy season

Most answering services bill by the minute or charge $7–$10 per call, so your bill balloons exactly when calls spike. Callvix keeps it flat: $2.99 per inbound call, no matter how long or complex — on a simple monthly base. No long-term contract.
