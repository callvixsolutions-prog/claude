How it works

Every call handled the way your own office would — just never missed

Callvix is a complete pest control call answering service. Trained people answer in your business name, apply your coverage areas, pricing, and rules, and book the job — like a back office you don't have to hire, train, or manage.

What's included

Four ways we keep your phone covered

Inbound call answering

Live answering in your company name for new and existing customers — every call opens exactly the way your office would open it.

Emergency call handling

Urgent pest issues — wasps, rodents, infestations — are recognized and handled by your escalation rules, so nothing time-sensitive gets stuck.

After-hours & overflow

Keep phones covered when your team is on a job, off the clock, or the office is simply closed for the day.

Appointment scheduling

Jobs booked directly on your availability, service areas, and pricing rules — logged clearly into your CRM for your team to action.

A trained person, not a bot
Who answers

A real person who understands pest control — not an automated menu

Every call is picked up by an agent trained specifically on pest control conversations. No "press 1 for scheduling." No script that falls apart when a caller goes off-topic.

They already know your services, your service area, your pricing, and your booking rules before they ever answer a call in your name — so the customer experiences your office, at its best, every time.
